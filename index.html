The studio

Small on purpose. Serious by design.

WestForge is a team of one: the same person plans the flow, designs the interface, writes the code, tunes the motion, and ships the site. No middle layer. No diluted taste. Just direct execution.

Strategy that becomes code

Every section has a job: explain, prove, convert, or guide. Nothing is added because it merely looks busy.

02

Motion with restraint

Animations support hierarchy and interaction. They feel expensive because they know when to stop.

03
